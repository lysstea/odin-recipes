White Peach Sangria

Description

A refreshing white wine sangria that is always a hit at parties.

Ingredients

  • 1 (750 milliliter) bottle dry white wine
  • 0.75 cups peach flavored vodka
  • 6 Tbsp frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
  • 0.25 cups white sugar
  • 1lb white peaches, pitted and sliced
  • 0.75 cups seedless red grapes, halved
  • 0.75 cups seedless green grapes, halved

Steps

  1. In a large pitcher, combine dry white whine, peach vodka, lemonade concentrate and sugar. Stir until sugar is dissolved. Add sliced peaches, red and green grapes.
  2. Refrigerate sangria until well chilled, at least 2 hours, or overnight to blend flavors. Serve over ice, and use a slotted spoon to include sliced peaches and grapes with each serving.
